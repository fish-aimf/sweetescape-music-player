# SweetEscape — A Full-Featured Music Player

> "Built with love and passion with JavaScript."

SweetEscape is an original, **Spotify replacement** — a static, full-featured music player made entirely with **HTML, CSS, and JavaScript**.
No frameworks. No backend servers. Just pure web magic.

It started as a small Glitch.com project and has since evolved into a fully functional feature packed music player — now hosted on **Vercel**.

---

## About the Project

SweetEscape is a **self-contained static website** that:

* Stores all your songs, settings, and playlists **locally** using **Session Storage**, **LocalStorage**, **IndexedDB** and even **Disk storage**.
* Allows you to **Search up any song you like and add it to your library**
* Speedily **download your favorite songs** and **Link them for offline playback**
* Can be accessed **offline** due to service worker.
* Doubles as a **karaoke player** and **lyrics transcriber**.
* Tracks **billboard hot-100** charts
* Is designed for **speed, simplicity, and beauty** — with a goal of hitting **1 million visits**. (3800 visits as of 10/16/2025) (7764 visits as of 11/20/2025) (10851 visits as of 6/27/2026)

> Once just a fun Glitch experiment, SweetEscape became something much more —
> a full-fledged, privacy-friendly, personal music world that runs entirely in your browser.

## Key Features

* **IndexedDB Library** for song storage
* **Recommendations** for songs in your library
* Lightning **fast search** for **any** song avaliable on Youtube.
* Search **filters** of containing lyrics, favorited and is downloaded.
* Display **Recently played Songs and Playlists**
* **Edit** and **Favorite** your songs in the library.
* **Completely Offline access** after first visit
* **Lightning fast download** any song to your device.
* **Link** offline files for **local** offline playback.
* Ability to **queue songs** and complex **actions**.
* Create your own **playlists** - temporarily **skip** songs.
* **Lyric transcriber & Karaoke Mode**
* **Preceise transcription** of songs to your liking.
* Automatic generation of lyrics when possible from **youtube subtitles**.
* **Share** your Songs and Karaoke with a URL with your friends.
* **Import and Export** your playlists, or even whole song library to share.
* **Looping** (Playlist or single), and **speed and volume** controls.
* App **Timer** to perform actions.
* **Listening time** Display.
* Connect to your **Discord Rich Presence** to share your activity with friends.
* **Dark/Light/Custom theme Mode Toggle**
* **Import/Export themes**
* Customizable **Hotkeys** for fast navigation
* **Billboard hot 100 updated and synced daily**
* **picture in picture** mode
* **Clean** collapasible/adjustable interface
* **Music Visaulizer**
* Adjust every **Setting** not to your liking
