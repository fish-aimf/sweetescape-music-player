# Code of Conduct

We want **SweetEscape Music Player** to be a welcoming and inclusive community for everyone.  
By participating in this project, you agree to follow this code of conduct.

---

## Be Respectful

- Treat all contributors with respect and kindness.  
- Be patient and constructive in feedback.  
- Avoid harassment, discrimination, or offensive language.

---

## Collaborate Positively

- Focus on ideas, not individuals.  
- Be open to different skill levels and experiences.

---

## Reporting Issues

If you experience or witness unacceptable behavior:  
- Open a private GitHub Issue labeled `code-of-conduct`  
- Or contact the repository owner directly

---

## Enforcement

Violations may result in warnings, removal from the project, or banning from discussions.  
The goal is to maintain a safe and collaborative environment for everyone.
